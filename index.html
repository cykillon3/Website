Projects

VRChat furry avatar with a hint of FNaF series

Goal:

The goal was to create an avatar from scratch without using any third-party assets.

Process:

I began with a reference sheet and a brief description of the avatar's functions. Using Blender, I modeled the base body of the avatar and collaborated with the client to make any necessary adjustments. After finalizing the model, I textured it in Substance Painter and then rigged it in Blender. Once the model was complete, I set it up in Unity for use in VRChat. The model is cross-platform compatible (PC and Oculus Quest).

Challenges:

One challenge I faced was that the client wanted the model to be grabbable by other players, allowing them to lift the avatar in-game. I achieved this by using Phys Bones in Unity, adding a toggle for Phys Bones in all parts of the armature. This was rooted to the handle bone on the back of the model, putting the avatar in a T-pose and allowing other players to grab it by its back handle.

About me.

Hi, I'm Talha, a self taught Vrchat Avatar creator and editor with a knack for bringing ideas to virtual world. I like creating anime style models with features as emotes, different outfits, weapons and much more, and I join my 3D modeling skills with web application development to create simple and unique 3D web apps.
